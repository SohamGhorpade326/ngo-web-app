From a Small Step to a Giant Leap

'Basti ki Pathshala' started with a simple idea: no child should be left behind. What began under a tree with a handful of students has grown into a community-led movement. We run makeshift schools in slums and remote areas, providing not just academic knowledge, but also values, nutrition, and a safe space to grow.

Our strength is our volunteers and the local community. We believe in empowering people to create change from within.
